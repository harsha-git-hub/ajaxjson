Title:Smart Home.
Description : The Smart Home focuses on providing intelligence to home wirelessly . Our project have three important features of a smart home such as Safety, Security & E-Services. The safety aspects deals with the monitoring of Smoke and LPG, thereby preventing the occurence of a Fire Accident. E-Services deals with a variety of applications one of them being the control of household electrical applications such as fans, lights, Air-Conditioner etc. with the help of a mobile application, it also provides other facility such as maintaining of records. the security aspects deals with the surveillance using a camera and indication in the event of an intrusion. Thus providing overall requirements for a home to be smart.
